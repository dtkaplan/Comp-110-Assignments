The Industrial Revolution

Fast Facts

  • The Industrial revolution occurred in both Europe and the United States.

  • The Industrial Revolution in London was especially detrimental to the public because of excessive pollution.

  • Many children were forced to work in factories during this time.

Effects of Industry

  1. People began to populate cities.

  2. Public health declined drastically.

  3. There was a rise in humanitarian organizations to improve poverty and working conditions.
