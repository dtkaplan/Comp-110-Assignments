Graphic 2

  • Graphic 2 is a density plot.
  • bmi, pregnant, and sex are involved.
  • bmi is on the x axis, pregnant is the faceet wrap, and sex maps its two categorical levels across the density plot.
  • There’s no data variable being used on the y axis here because density is a function that’s working on bmi, calculating the density of its various values across NCHS.
ggplot(data = CPS85, aes(x = exper, y = wage, color = married)) + geom_point() + facet_wrap( ~ sector)
